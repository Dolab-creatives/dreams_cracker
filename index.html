    Fireworks Safety Tips

  • Fireworks/Firecrackers are strictly for outdoor use purpose only, Never fire fireworks inside the house, buildings, or any-other places.
  • All fireworks to be fired under Adult supervision.
  • Before fire a fireworks, kindly read and follow the instructions given on the pack and fire with care.
  • Before fire a fireworks, better to have a enough water in bucket and sand bucket in case of accidents.
  • Don’t fire all crackers at a time, fire one by one
  • Don’t ever try to make fireworks at home.
  • It always advisable to wear cotton cloths when firing crackers, fireworks
  • Always safe to wash your hands after firing fireworks.
